About Us

Stop putting off renovations in your home for a rainy day. You need an expert contractor by your side that knows exactly what they’re doing and can get the job done on time and within your budget. At R T R Carpentry & Roofing Ltd, we have been providing quality and affordable interior and exterior renovations, construction services, and roofing installations across Fort St John and the surrounding areas since 1998.

Catering to homes of all shapes and sizes, our team has used their 26 years of experience in the field to modernize client’s kitchens and bathrooms, transform unused basements into family rooms, add curb appeal to homes with new roofing, siding, decks and so much more.

From day one, we listen to your design visions, creating a custom space that exceeds all of your expectations. All of our contractors are licensed and insured members of the Fort St John Chamber of Commerce. Give us a call or send us an email to request a quote.
